Hearty Vegetable Soup

Home

Description

This comforting vegetable soup is packed with fresh veggies and warming herbs.

Perfect for a cozy meal any day of the week, and great for leftovers!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup chopped spinach or kale

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic; saut√© until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add carrots, celery, and zucchini; cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, thyme, and basil.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Add spinach or kale; cook until wilted, about 3 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.
