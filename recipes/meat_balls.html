Meat Balls

Home

Description

This classic meatball recipe delivers juicy, flavorful bites packed with herbs and spices.

Perfect for pasta, subs, or as a hearty appetizer the whole family will love.

Ingridients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, egg, garlic, salt, pepper, oregano, and milk.
  3. Mix everything gently until just combined—avoid overmixing.
  4. Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 to 1.5 inches in diameter.
  5. Place the meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until cooked through and browned.
  7. Remove from oven and serve hot with your favorite sauce or pasta.
